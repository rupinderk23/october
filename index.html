This month we bring to the spotlight a dynamic musical duo - Clive Vaz and Rahul Bakshi! Specializing in an instrumental fusion set that blends a rich fusion of sounds, this pair creates an unforgettable auditory experience.
Clive and Rahul
A Musical Duo Fusing East and West
Clive Vaz & Rahul Bakshi form a powerful duo ensemble that creates a unique blend of live electronica and world fusion music. Clive displays his mastery over a diverse range of instruments, including the bansuri, didgeridoo, mouth-harp, hulusi, flutes, and various percussion instruments. Rahul complements Clive’s melodies with his impressive percussion skills, playing the Dhol, Tabla, Djembe, Darbuka, Didgeridoo, and Kajoo. Together, they offer a captivating fusion of traditional and contemporary sounds, making their performances not just an interesting musical journey but also a beautifully immersive experience.

Kolkata Metro Celebrates 40 Years with Iconic Performances by Ustad Amjad Ali Khan and Shubha Mudgal

Ustad Amjad Ali Khan

Shubha Mudgal

In celebration of 40 years of Metro Railway in Kolkata, India’s first underground metro and a symbol of urban transformation, a special cultural programme was held featuring live performances by two of the country’s most revered artists. Padma Vibhushan Ustad Amjad Ali Khan, an internationally acclaimed sarod maestro known for his soulful renditions, and Padma Shri Shubha Mudgal, celebrated for her powerful voice and contributions to Hindustani classical music, were chosen to honor this significant milestone. Both artists performed individually, presenting audiences with unique expressions of India’s rich musical heritage. Their performances reflected the essence of Kolkata’s deep-rooted cultural traditions, creating an atmosphere of pride and nostalgia as Metro Railway marked four decades of service in the City of Joy.

Navigating Digital Transformation: Influencers and Matrimony Experts Share Insights at Standard Chartered’s Enterprise Leaders Program

Sharan Hegde, Sarthak Ahuja, Neha Nagar, and Revant Himatsingka

Sima Taparia, Priyanka Biswas, Elixir Nahar

Standard Chartered’s Enterprise Leaders Program in Mumbai brought together senior leaders from around the world to explore the intersections of banking, social media, and matchmaking in today’s digital age. The event featured a dynamic panel of famous influencers, including Sharan Hegde, Sarthak Ahuja, Neha Nagar, and Revant Himatsingka, who shared insights into the rapidly expanding $22 billion influencer industry. They discussed the impact they have on audiences, how they build their fanbases, and the strategies behind creating powerful, short-form content that resonates with followers and prompts action. The influencers also offered advice on reaching clients effectively in a competitive digital space.
In a unique addition, the program hosted a panel with top matrimony experts, including Sima Taparia, India’s famed matchmaker; Priyanka Biswas from Shaadi.com, and Elixir Nahar from Bumble. They discussed shifts in client behavior, the role of technology in matchmaking, and the importance of creating user-friendly digital experiences. Together, these discussions provided a compelling exchange on how digital and social trends are reshaping industries from finance to relationships.

Inspiring Diwali Insights with Yuvraj Singh at HSBC Singapore

Yuvraj Singh

HSBC Singapore recently hosted an exclusive Diwali event for HNI customers and prospects, featuring a compelling discussion with former cricketer and humanitarian Yuvraj Singh, moderated by BBC journalist Sharanjit Leyl. Focused on the topic “Live Your Better Future,” the conversation delved into Yuvraj’s evolution from sports legend to resilient cancer survivor and philanthropist. He shared perspectives on balancing health, wealth, and family, reflecting on how travel shaped his worldview and how he envisions a lasting legacy. His experiences with the YouWeCan Foundation resonated with guests, emphasizing resilience, community, and the importance of giving back.

Asian Paints Unveils New Range in Style

Aayana Dance Group
